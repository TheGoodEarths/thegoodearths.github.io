Want a glimpse into how a small mill manufactures coconut oil? Stay with us as we explain.

We source the coconuts from the customers directly or through our trusted vendors if our customer seeks it. After opening and draining the coconut water, the coconut are dried faced down for 2 hours and then sun dried for 3 days, when the outer shell comes loose. The coconut flesh at this stage is called Copra. The inner flesh is cut into 1 inch pieces and further dried for about a week or two before it becomes suitable for oil extraction. The coconut pieces are checked for fungus or impurities. Store it in a good air tight container or take it to small extraction unit to extract oil immediately.

During extraction, the oil will be cloudy, allow it to settle for 1 day and you will have sparkling clean oil. Transfer the clean oil from the top to a different container. Cloudy and creamy residue at the base is highly nutritious and should not be wasted. It can be used to lighting lamps, massaging your body, even cooking gravy, chaka and can be consumed as it is. We recommend that you use the creamy layer in a couple of days. We striclty adhere above to delivery the best coconut oil for you

This process is time consuming, hard work and patience. However it’s worth the effort if you care for your health and sustainable living.
