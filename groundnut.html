How groundnut oil is extracted

We extract oil from best quality of Groundnut(Mittai parupu) , which is handpicked and shell is broken with full nut to extract the oil.

Peanut is also known as goober, pinder, groundnut, earthnut and ground pea. Groundnut oil is natural sweet and flavoured edible oil. unrefined Groundnut oil has a light yellow color and flavored taste of groundnut. This is extracted from pressing groundnut kernels. In addition to extracting the oil, groundnuts are eaten raw or roasted, and used in cooking sweets and bakery products as well. Groundnut oil is very high on energy, has a very high burning and smoking point, and contains monounsaturated fats, Vitamins A, D and E. Once extracted we leave it sun dry for a day to observe the vitamins.
